Our Facilities

Embark on an educational journey surrounded by innovation at AGS URI. Our facilities, from cutting-edge Computer Lab to modern study spaces, are meticulously designed to foster success and excellence. Explore, engage, and excel in our world-class facilities.

Best Library in Uri

The school has a well stocked library comprising of General references on every subject, books on General knowledge, encyclopedia , magazines on sports , digital technology etc.The Library has a collection of approx 3500 books.Library books should be obtained only with the permission of the Librarian..

Largest Play Ground

Competitive sports and physical education are important aspects of life. Army Goodwill School, Uri emphasis on both physical fitness as well as skill at games. Boxing, Hockey, Basketball, Volleyball and Cricket grounds are available to the students to develop their skill in these games.

Tuck Shop

The school has a tuck shop to cater for day-to-day requirements of the staff and students. Tuck- shop provides snacks, biscuits, soft drinks, ice-cream and other quailty products as per the approved rates of the school Management Committee. Quality Control Commitee of the school carries surprise checks to ensure the maintenance of quality and hygiene of the tuck shop.
